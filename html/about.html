ABOUT US

Our Story

Welcome to Bordentown Bagels, your friendly neighborhood bagel shop in Bordentown, New Jersey. We've been serving our community since 1993, and we're proud to be female-owned and locally operated. Our bagels are made from scratch, hand-rolled, boiled, and baked to perfection. We offer a variety of classic and specialty bagels, each crafted with care and filled with flavor.

In addition to bagels, we create our own flavored butters, cream cheeses, and salads right here on-site. From traditional spreads like plain cream cheese to unique flavors like honey pecan, our selection has something for everyone. Our salads, including chicken, egg, and tuna, are made with fresh ingredients and a touch of love.

At Bordentown Bagels, customer service is our top priority. We aim to create a warm and welcoming environment where you can enjoy a delicious meal with friends and family. Whether you're stopping by for a quick breakfast or settling in for lunch, we're here to make your visit memorable.

We're proud to serve Burlington and Mercer counties and appreciate the ongoing support from our community. Visit us today to experience the best bagels in town!
